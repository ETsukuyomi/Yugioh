OCG Meta

OCG refers to "Original Card Game" which is played in the Asia region. They have earlier access to cards
while having a different banlist. The business model that OCG uses is to have a variety of decks be competitive,
be affordable, and is built around the player base rather than money.
This content is based off RoadoftheKing and their reports, as tournament
information from the Asian tournaments are difficult to obtain and be translated.
